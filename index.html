The short version

I spent two years at Google shipping features to a billion users. Then I went to the UN to build a climate data portal. Then I joined RMSI and shipped two government apps from scratch for the state of Assam. Somewhere along the way, I stopped caring about the code and started caring about the problem. Now I'm looking for PM roles where I can bring both.

The longer version

Nov 2024 – Mar 2026 · 1 yr 5 mos · Noida, India

The honest part: half the job was sitting in rooms with non-technical stakeholders, figuring out what they actually needed versus what was written in the brief. That's the work that made me want to be a PM.

Why PM, why now

Every role I've had has pulled me closer to the problem and further from the terminal. At Google I learned scale. At the UN I learned stakeholder alignment. At RMSI I learned what 0-to-1 actually feels like when the users are forest rangers in Assam with intermittent connectivity. I don't want to go back to just building. I want to own the what and why.
